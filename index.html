About GSY Fuel Prices

GSY Fuel Prices tracks petrol and diesel prices at forecourts across Guernsey, updated weekly by hand. CIFL stations tend to change prices on Mondays and Tuesdays, Rubis stations on Fridays. The prices shown are what you actually pay at the pump, not including any loyalty card discounts.

Guernsey sets its own fuel duty, currently 90.5p per litre for road fuel and 60.5p for RD100, which is why prices here don't follow the mainland directly. We also keep an eye on Brent Crude so you can see what's driving any changes at the forecourt.

Seen a price that's out of date? Hit the Report a price button and send us a photo of the sign. We check every submission and get the listing updated. Heating oil and bulk marine fuel rates are listed too, for anyone who needs them.
