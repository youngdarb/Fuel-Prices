Guernsey Fuel Forecourts

Every petrol station on the island, with supplier info and fuel types

There are around 20 active fuel forecourts in Guernsey. Most are supplied by one of three main distributors: CIFL, Rubis, or GPD. The supplier largely determines when prices change and what loyalty schemes are available.

CIFL-supplied stations typically update prices on Mondays and Tuesdays. Rubis stations update on Fridays. Some forecourts also sell premium grades such as Super Unleaded (RON 97 or RON 99) or biofuel alternatives.

Prices at the pump include Guernsey road fuel duty, currently set at 90.5p per litre. This is separate from UK fuel duty and is one reason island prices move independently from the mainland.

Fuel Types Available in Guernsey

Unleaded (E5/E10) — Standard petrol sold at all road forecourts. Some ATF stations sell E10 (10% ethanol blend). E10 is not suitable for all older vehicles, so check your handbook before using it.

Diesel (B7) — Standard diesel, sometimes sold as a B7 blend (up to 7% biodiesel). ATF stations sell B7. Check vehicle compatibility if your car is older than 2000.

Super Unleaded (RON 97/99) — Available at seven forecourts. Suitable for high-performance engines and older cars that require higher-octane fuel. Note: Excellium (RON 95) is not Super Unleaded.

Bio/HVO — Hydrotreated Vegetable Oil, a renewable fuel alternative. Available at Camp du Roi and Jeffrey's Service Station, and through Rubis/Forest Supplies heating oil.

DF Diesel (Dyed Fuel) — Duty-free diesel for off-road and marine use. Not legal for use in road vehicles. Available at several marine and specialist outlets.

Marine Unleaded — Supplied at Boatworks+ and QEII Marina for leisure craft. Marine petrol duty in Guernsey is currently 61.4p per litre (as of January 2026).
